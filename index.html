Description

This service is for the installation of one indoor gas stove only.

The service is valid for areas within a 45km radius of City centres in Johannesburg, Pretoria, Cape Town, and Durban.

The solution will be provided by 4-Sure Technology Solutions and their network of approved, skilled and accredited service providers to perform installations on the terms, conditions and rates set out below and subject to the service terms and conditions.

Included in this purchase

  • 1x PingGo installation service for gas stove
  • Up to 5 metres of piping
  • Up to 5 metres of electrical cable
  • Installation and connection of the pre-purchased safety / cut-off valve
  • Certificate of compliance issued by LPGSA
  • All services are redeamable through PingGo!

What's not included

  • Gas bottle
  • Regulator valve
  • Safety/shut off valve
  • External safety cage if required
  • Cutting or re-surfacing of marble, granite, masonite tabletops or cabinetry
  • Travel over and above a 45km radius of city centres in Johannesburg, Pretoria, Cape Town, and Durban. Additional travel will be charged at R4.00 per additional km greater than 45km

How to redeem this service

  1. After completing your purchase, you will receive a unique service link redeemable through PingGo!.
  2. Click on the link “book appointment” or alternatively you can get access to all your recent service purchases from the orders tab. After choosing to book for an installation the PingGo! app will be opened. Use of PingGo is subject to the terms of use made available on your registration as a user.
  3. This service is valid for thirty-six (36) months from the date of purchase.

What's not included

  • The area where the gas stove needs to be placed must be easily accessible
  • The gas stove must be the correct size for the intended location
  • Any work over and above the inclusions in this service will be quoted by the service provider, to be settled directly with the service provider.
  • The installer will be a third-party contractor assigned by 4-Sure Technology Solutions (Proprietary) Limited. The contractor is solely responsible for the performance of the services and will be liable for any damages, losses or claims which may arise as a result thereof.
  • External safety cage if required

As noted above, use of PingGo is subject to the PingGo terms of use made available on your registration as a user.
